Question: I can't click the "Continue" button because the window contents don't fit within the window itself, and the button is out of sight. What should I do?
Answer: This happens occasionally on poorly configured operating systems. There's a TAB key on your keyboard. Use it to navigate to the desired menu item.

Question: When I launch the installation, I get the error "SSE 4.2 or later." What should I do?
Answer: According to Adobe , Photoshop 2021 and later requires a processor with support for SSE 4.2 or later. Unfortunately, if your processor doesn't support this architecture, this version of Photoshop will not run on your computer.

Question: When opening a program, I get the error "Adobe Creative Cloud, needed to resolve the problem, is missing or damaged...". What should I do?
Answer: You have leftover installations of Adobe Creative Cloud components on your system. These need to be removed. To do this, use the Adobe Creative Cloud Cleaner Tool(https://swupmf.adobe.com/webfeed/CleanerTool/win/AdobeCreativeCloudCleanerTool.exe) . Select "All" as the component to remove, then "Adobe Creative Cloud Desktop". Then, reinstall the offending program. This cleanup and reinstallation will not damage any previously installed programs.

Question: I want to use Photoshop with two or more interface languages ??and be able to switch between them natively. Is this possible?
Answer: Reinstall the program in a different language directly over the existing one. After that, you'll be able to change the interface language using the native switch in the menu "Edit->Preferences->Interface...->UI Language."
